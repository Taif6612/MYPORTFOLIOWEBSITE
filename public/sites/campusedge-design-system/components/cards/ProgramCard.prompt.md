Grid tile for an academic program. Image zooms and card lifts on hover.

```jsx
<ProgramCard
  image="https://images.unsplash.com/photo-..."
  title="Computer Science"
  degree="B.Sc"
  duration="4 Years"
  blurb="Algorithms, AI, and systems with a year in industry."
  icon="fa-solid fa-microchip"
/>
```

Use in a responsive `repeat(auto-fill, minmax(300px, 1fr))` grid. Keep blurbs to one line.
