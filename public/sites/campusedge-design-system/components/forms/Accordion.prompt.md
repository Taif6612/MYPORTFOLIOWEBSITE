FAQ / disclosure list. One panel open at a time.

```jsx
<Accordion items={[
  { q: 'What are the admission requirements?', a: 'A completed secondary diploma…' },
  { q: 'Do you offer scholarships?', a: 'Yes — merit and need-based awards…' },
]} />
```

The plus toggle rotates to an ×. Pass `defaultOpen={-1}` to start fully collapsed.
