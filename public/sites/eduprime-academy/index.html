Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything parents and students want to know before enrolling.

What are the course fees?

Fees vary by program, ranging from ৳8,000 for short courses to ৳45,000 for full admission packages. Flexible monthly installments and sibling discounts are available — contact us for a personalized quote.

How are the class schedules arranged?

Most batches run 3 days a week with weekend options for working students and college-goers. You can choose morning, afternoon or evening slots based on availability at enrollment.

Do you offer online classes?

Yes. Every course is available in-person and live online, with recorded lectures so you never miss a session. Online students get the same study materials and mock tests.

Are scholarships available?

We offer merit-based and need-based scholarships covering up to 75% of tuition. A short scholarship assessment determines eligibility — ask our counselors during admission.

What is the admission process?

Simply fill the inquiry form or call us. We schedule a free counseling session and assessment, recommend the right batch, and complete enrollment in a single visit.
